About Me


Experience spanning 10+ years in coordination and management roles in film production, private country club operations, non-profits, and SME workflow automations. Recently upgraded my skills by completing an AEC career diploma in Full Stack Development (2023-24), and returned to complete my previous BFA Film Production at Concordia University (2025) — open to project and operations roles from May 2025.

Here are some things about me that may not have made it onto the CV. I previously worked in theatre as an actor in Canada and the UK. I took up martial arts at a young age, taught for a several years, and did performance work on motion capture for Ubisoft (Rocky Balboa). After working as a performer for 7 years, I studied film production at Concordia University focusing on production management and student leadership roles over 3 years (organizing a 5-day film festival, workshops, and industry partnerships; faculty board committee roles for Cinema Chair Hiring, Department Appraisal Committee). On my free time: I train in amateur boxing, I love playing music (currently classical guitar and starting on Arabic oud), and I volunteer as a dog foster.

I bring all of these experiences with me, along with training in full stack development, to my work on projects and operations. I am consistently learning new skills and figuring out how to solve new problems — taking a course or workshop in my spare time. Reach out if you have a project you're working on or are looking for dynamic solutions for your business or team — looking forward to talking with you!
